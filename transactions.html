Admin login

All transactions

Each user's listings collapse into their own section — flagged (disputed) sections open automatically. Disputed listings sort to the top within a section: Approve keeps them and clears the flag, Change edits quantity, value, and date, Void hides them, and Delete removes them permanently.

Admin access is enforced by Firebase Auth and Firestore rules.
